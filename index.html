Biography

I'm a current junior at Tufts University studying Mechanical and Biomedical Engineering. I have worked on a range of projects, from molding and casting prosthetics to building soft-robot prototypes for natural disaster relief, or building an assistive device for a boy with ectrodactyly. In my future, I would like to create innovative products that enhance the daily lives of individuals, strengthen human resilience to injury, or combat climate change.
